About Us

SEAM Consulting - a consulting company based in Vilnius, Lithuania, providing high-quality services for clients from all over the world. Our team has bulletproof experience and know-how in the field of banking sector - especially with fintechs and the emerging market of blockchain and crypto. We are also proud of our direct access to an unprecedented number of key players from Lithuania ( as well as other EU countries), Asia, the Gulf Region and the United Kingdom.
We provide our Clients 360° support in most pressing situations and adapt our deliverables to the needs of each individual Client. Our team is goal orientated, always gives candid and objective feedback that allows our Clients to make the toughest decisions easier and capitalise on the market opportunities.
This is extremely important when working with complex industries like the banking sector or blockchain, dealing with public institutions and approaching potential clients. On top of the existing know-how we constantly follow the pulse of the above listed industries to provide our Clients up to date information and guidance - this is crucial for this fast pace and constantly developing world.
